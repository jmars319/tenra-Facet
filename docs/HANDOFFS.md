# Facet by Tenra Handoffs

Facet by Tenra stays unique as a shared orientation app. Its job is to help a person see a question from multiple angles without answering for them.

## Produces

- `tenra-facet.orientation-packet.v1` JSON.
- Derive prompts that preserve the original question, surfaced perspectives, result provenance, and follow-up angles.

## Consumes

- Local notes, documents, and sanitized search/source context.
- Optional downstream feedback about which orientation packets became useful Derive, Assembly, Sentinel, or Scout work.

Facet should not become an answer engine or risk oracle. Send answer work to Derive and evidence-weighted risk work to Sentinel.
